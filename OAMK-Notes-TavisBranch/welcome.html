Welcome

Hello there and welcome to OAMK Note! This platform is intended for students at OAMK, to write and save notes that they make during their lectures and studies. It allows you the user, to store your notes in an organized and easy manner. You can also set homework/task deadlines for yourself.

Stay tuned for more features!
